- *Connected* — one properly integrated platform; agentic; plays well with 3rd-party tools.
- *Scales* — works for small and extra-large businesses; role-based permissions built in.
- *Fast to deploy* — one-click import of customers/properties/materials; pre-loaded rate cards, calculators, templates; no hours of videos/meetings.

**Our Approach / differentiation (Legacy stack vs 3Maples)**

- Disconnected & confusing → Connected end-to-end
- Months to implement → Live in under 10 minutes
