### 2026-05-30 — Profit Leakage & Unbillable Time

**Category:** Profit Leakage / Labor Burden
**Format:** Educational / value tip

Keep more of the profit you work so hard for. Avoid profit leakage by creating more complete estimates that include every aspect of effort required to deliver a job — materials, labor, overhead, profit margin.

One of the most overlooked killers: **unbillable time**. Paid labor that produces no directly billable revenue:
- Travel time
- Loading and unloading
- Waiting on site
- Admin and paperwork
- Rework and callbacks
- Material pickup runs
- Poor dispatch

Most owners dramatically underestimate how much this costs. It lowers utilization, raises your effective labor burden, and quietly erodes your margins job by job. Let Maple do the hard work and show you where it's happening.
